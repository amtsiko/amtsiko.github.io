Process Services

Overview

PLACEHOLDER: The University of Michigan Lurie Nanofabrication Facility (LNF) includes 11,000 sq. ft. of class 10/100/1000 cleanroom and 2,500 sq. ft/ of quasi class 10,000 space with a BiosSafety Level 2 space. The LNF provides a wide range of fabrication and characterization capabilities and our highly experienced scientists and engineers are here to help you with your process needs. The LNF offers process services to researchers unable to come work onsite themselves.

Here are examples of services that we offer

  • E-beam lithography
  • DRIE
  • Wafer Bonding
  • Thermal Processes
  • Soft Lithography
  • Analytical Services
  • Thin Films

and many more!
